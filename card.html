Collie's

Collie puppies look like tiny balls of fluff until they grow into their adult coats at around six months old.

  • Weight: 31-44 lbs
  • Lifespan: 10-14 years
  • Backstory: They are a working and herding dog breed developed in England's Scottish-bordering county of Northumberland, for herding livestock, especially sheep.
  • Care: Brush his/her coat at least weekly and brush his/her teeth at least twice a week!

A dog breed that's well-known for herding and protecting abilities, rough collie dogs are described as strong, loyal, affectionate, responsive, and fast.
